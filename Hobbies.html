My Hobbies

Some of my hobbies are dance, swim, badminton, and reading.

The main form of dance I take is ballet. I have been doing ballet for about 13 years. During the school year, I usually dance for 10-15 hours a week. I have classes, variations, and rehearsals throughout the week and my classes can range from 1-4 hours a day. Each year my studio holds two prodictions and one end-of-year performance.

I used to do swim team for a while but then I stopped two years ago. Even though I stopped swimming competitively I still enjoy swimming for fun here and there.
